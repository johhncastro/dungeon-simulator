Ghost

The Ghost is a very mystical creature that stays hidden but will reveal if threatened. Many townsman believe they have seen this ghost coming back from the Hunt. They say Ghost lurks and stalks people who wander through the woods but will never strike. They say Ghost feeds on FEAR!

Abilities

Ghost Slash

Phase Wave

Bubble Shield

The Ghost slash is swift most reliable in a pinch it does average DMG it also rarely misses. This attack can do up to 3 DMG with a range of 0-3 DMG (this is base dmg with no buffs or leveling

The Swing Staff attack is uses when in a pinch and mage needs some serious damage done to the enemy. This attack is very risky because it has a 60% chance to miss the target and when it does land it takes 1 health however this attack does 6 DMG if it hits.

The Heal Ability is unique and special to mage because he is the only character that can heal without needing a healing potion. However, this takes a turn and Mage will not be able to attack that round. Mage heals 2 HP
